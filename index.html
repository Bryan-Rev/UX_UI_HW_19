Hello! I'm Bryan.

I am a UX/UI designer that specializes in UI design, user research, and prototyping for mobile and desktop designs. I enjoy solving user problems that are a part of their day to day life. My skills include Figma and critical thinking.

About Me

As someone who grew up loving to build and create things I would have never known how much I would enjoy creating digitally. I went from being that usual college student that didn’t quite know what they wanted to do to being a disciplined designer pursuing the UX/UI field.

Designing UI that is user friendly and visually appealing is such a rewarding feeling that increases my passion and desire for the field. There is so much more to learn in the UX/UI world and I am eager to push myself and connect with others in the industry.
